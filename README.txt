Requirements for play:

No Python Extensions Needed

================================================================================

Here we have a game similar to Choose Your Own Adventure. However, you
have 4 paths to choose from. Feel free to continue down each path to see the
different options you can choose, and the differrent experiences that lie await.

The Following Experiences Include: 

1) Apocalyptic Wasteland Experience: New Cutting Edge Dive Technology has been
made, and you are invited to test it out. Your main goal, is to survive.

2) Superhuman Experience: You are given the choice between Mind Reading and
Strength. Both leading to different experiences.

3) College Experience: Life during COVID-19 is tiredsome. Live the life of a
College student having to make some choices.

4) Cabin Horror Experience: You and some friends decide to go on vacation to
a cabin for a few weeks. Everything is great until things start becoming
errie. Can you survive?
